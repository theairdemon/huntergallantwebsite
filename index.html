About Me


For years, I've been fascinated by computers, and in particular, artificial intelligence. What would it be like to have a conversation with a true artificial intelligence? When techology reaches that point, where we define it as being "alive", there will be an incredible change in the state of the human condition. This new form of life, created by humanity, will be fundamentally different than any form of biological life that currently exists.

I, for one, am very excited for this day to arrive. I want to do everything I can to contribute to this future, to the creation of a true artifical intelligence. Most likely, we will not see this within the 21st century. Until then, we can take small steps, with each victory bringing us closer to the creation of new life.

This is my vision for the future. I am fascinated by how technology can be applied to activities we consider distinctly human - poetry, art, music, and more. My projects reflect my ambitions, and although they are nowhere near perfect, I am constantly improving them.
